Student Clubs

Kalanjali Commitee

Pullaiah College of Engineering and Technology has always strived for holistic development of its pupil. To develop leadership skills in the students, GPCET conducts extra- curricular activities. This development is very important, as it helps the student to be aware of his/her strengths.

Coding Club

Gpcet Provides a platform to the students to enhance coding and logical thinking skills. It Encourages the students to participate coding contests. Coding club regularly organizes counseling sessions or mentor sessions to counsel all the beginners and other members of coding club to guide what is right path to practice coding.

Pheonix Club

Pheonix Club is an English Language Club that was introduced with an intention of facilitating GPCET students with the activities that enhance student’s skills. Besides this, the club also encourages the students to use language to voice their point of view, involve in a regulated discussion, and finally demonstrate critical thinking.

Anveshna

Placing Win to Win Attitude as GPCET’s objective, Anveshana club has been started with the objective of exploring hidden technologies in engineering and technology by promoting research and innovative activities among the students and faculty. Since its inception, this club is actively working to fulfill its objective by conducting various activities for both the students and faculty.
